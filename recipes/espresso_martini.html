Espresso Martini

HISTORY

Created by the legendary Dick Bradsell in 1983, you can read the full story behind this popular cocktail, much of it in Dick's own words, on our Espresso Martini encyclopedia page.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ounces of Vodka
  • 1/2 ouce of coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce freshly brewed espresso
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • Coffee Beans

STEPS

  1. Add Vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso and simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice and shake until well-chilled
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass
  3. Garnish with 3 cofee beans
