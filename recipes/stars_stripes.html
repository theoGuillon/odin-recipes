Stars & Stripes

HISTORY

Pay homage to the colors of the holiday with this fruity layered cocktail. Muddled blueberries lend blue to the drink, a drizzle of raspberry brandy provides a red hue, and the sugar cube garnish gives a white touch and a burst of sweetness to the drink.

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 fresh bluberries
  • 1 ounce of blueberry Vodka/li>
  • 1/4 ounce absinthe
  • 1/4 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 drizzle raspberry brandy

STEPS

  1. In a shaker, muddle the blueberries
  2. Add the Vodka, absinthe, lemon juice and simple syrup with ice and shake until well-chilled
  3. Pour (unstrained) into a higball glass
  4. Drizzle with raspberry brandy
  5. Garnish with ice cube
